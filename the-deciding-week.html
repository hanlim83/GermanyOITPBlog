The Deciding Week (Week 3, 18 - 24 March)

This is the week where the test of our independence starts coming in. Our fellow friends left and went back to Singapore on 16th March 2019 and our grind officially begins. We felt a feeling that we had never felt before, after our friends left. I feel that we were a little home-sick after being with more companions and having so much fun together and that when they left. We felt isolated. However, we told ourselves that we have another mission that we need to complete which was to complete our internship with flying colors!
