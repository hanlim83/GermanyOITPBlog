This week was the final week of our project! We had to submit everything by Tuesday, the 14 of May. It was quite the stressful experience but we managed to get everything done and dusted before the dateline! The reason why the dateline was on a Tuesday was because our final meeting with the Professor falls on a Wednesday and he wanted sometime to look at it before grading us the next day. Oh yes, in case we did not introduce to you our professor. He is Prof. Dr Christian Pape.
keyboard_arrow_left Previous keyboard_arrow_right Next
Initially, at the start we thought that he was a very strict person. However, as the weeks go past, we managed to find out a lot of things about him and Germany. He is actually extremely helpful in many ways. For example in project wise, whenever we have any questions, he will always try his best to help us.
Apart from project, he also helped us to know more about the university, places to go and food to eat in Germany! When he knew that we were going to Switzerland, he did not hesitate to help us to plan where is a MUST to go. The project meeting were usually about an hour long, he was not entitled to help us but he used his own time to help us plan for the Switzerland trip. It lasted for close to 3 hours!

2-HOUR LONG PLANNING

In this week post, we would want to sincerely thank our professor. Thank you Dr. Christian Pape for spending the time and effort to guide us in this 12-week journey! It was really an eye-opening experience for the both of us!

SWITZERLAND !!

For this entire Switzerland trip, we stayed in Bern! It was not far from the city center and we got the explore a lot in this city itself!
We planned to explore each city in a day respectively so we got right at it as soon as we checked into our hotel!
  • For the first day, we stayed within Bern, Switzerland.
  • For the second day, we went to Zurich, Switzerland.
  • For the third day, we went to Interlaken, Switzerland.
  • For the last day, we went to Basel, Switzerland.
